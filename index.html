Travel & Accommodation

Getting to the church: If driving, the easiest place to park is the nearby Crown Court car park, where the church is accessible via a footpath. Alternative parking can be found at the Borough Council car park, or at the Train Station car park if needed. The nearest train station is Godalming, which is a short walk to the church.
Getting to the reception: The reception will be held at Oxenford Farm, which is approximately a 10 minute drive from the church. Please let us know if you would like to be included in a car share from the church to the reception when you RSVP. Parking is available in a field on site. Please follow sat-nav directions to: Oxenford Farm, Milford Road, Elstead, Surrey, GU8 6LA
Getting back: Unless you're fortunate enough to have a designated driver, we recommended you book a taxi in advance for your return journey. Local taxi companies include Surrey Cars, Surrey Hills Taxis, and Charterhouse Cars. Sadly, Uber is not available here. The last train from Godalming to London is at 23:45PM, later trains are availble until 1:20AM from Guildford. Please check the schedule on the day as these times may change.
